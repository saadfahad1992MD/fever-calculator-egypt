# Ibuprofen Syrup Medications Available in Egypt

## Research Date: November 8, 2025

## Popular Ibuprofen Syrups Found:

### 1. **Brufen (بروفين)**
- **Manufacturer:** Abbott
- **Concentration:** 100mg/5ml
- **Package Size:** 110ml, 150ml
- **Price:** ~28 EGP (150ml)
- **Status:** Very popular, widely available
- **Source:** Egyptian Drug Store, Bloom Pharmacy

### 2. **Profinal (بروفينال)**
- **Manufacturer:** Unknown (needs verification)
- **Concentration:** 100mg/5ml (20mg/ml)
- **Package Size:** 110ml
- **Status:** Popular alternative to Brufen
- **Source:** Adam Online, Aldawaeya Pharmacy, Point Pharmacies

### 3. **Nurofen (نيوروفين)**
- **Manufacturer:** Reckitt Benckiser
- **Concentration:** 100mg/5ml
- **Package Size:** 100ml
- **Status:** International brand, available in Egypt
- **Flavors:** Strawberry, Orange
- **Source:** Ubuy Egypt, Instagram posts

### 4. **Ibuflam N (ايبوفلام)**
- **Manufacturer:** Hikma
- **Concentration:** 120mg/5ml
- **Package Size:** 120ml
- **Price:** ~7 EGP
- **Status:** Available, budget-friendly
- **Source:** Egyptian Drug Store

### 5. **Ibugesic (ايبوجيسيك)**
- **Type:** Combination product (Ibuprofen + Paracetamol)
- **Concentration:** Ibuprofen 100mg + Paracetamol 125mg per 5ml
- **Status:** Available in some markets, needs verification for Egypt
- **Source:** Shalina Healthcare

## Notes:
- Standard concentration for children's ibuprofen syrup: **100mg/5ml**
- Some variations: 120mg/5ml (Ibuflam N)
- Age indication: Usually 6 months and above
- All are oral suspensions/syrups
